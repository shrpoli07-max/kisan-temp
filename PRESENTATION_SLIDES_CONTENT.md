## Slide 3: The Solution - Contextual Intelligence
**Headline:** Dynamic Global State Engine 
**Slide Content:**

- **The Schemes Matcher Engine:** We ingest real land size and regional data. The AI dynamically calculates and filters out irrelevant noise to surface only what the farmer strictly qualifies for (e.g., locking *YSR Rythu Bharosa* specifically to Andhra Pradesh).
- **Business Impact:** Billions in unallocated government subsidies finally reach the right demographics without bureaucratic red tape.

## Slide 4: Tech Innovation - True Multimodal Voice AI
**Headline:** Breaking the Literacy Barrier with Multimodal AI
**Slide Content:**
- **The Problem with Legacy Tech:** Native browser speech-to-text APIs crash on secure browsers (Brave/Web3) and struggle with rural Indian dialects.
- **Our Engineering Bypass:** We engineered a custom pipeline that streams raw `webm` audio blobs directly into **Google Gemini 2.5 Flash**.
- **The Result:** 100% browser-agnostic, zero-shot audio translation. The AI "hears" the native dialect (Telugu/Hindi), processes the request, updates the transcript live on the interface, and replies using native Text-to-Speech arrays. Zero typing required by the farmer.
